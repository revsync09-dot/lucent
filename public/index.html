Regulations

Helper Rules & Guidelines

All Helpers are expected to follow these rules to maintain quality service.

📜 Standard Rules

Priority: Handle tickets in chronological order. 15-minute response window before skip.

Integrity: Only claim missions you intend to complete. Avoid cross-trading or payment requests.

Handover: If unable to continue, unclaim immediately and notify the Helper channel.

💳 Payment

Follow carry-information strictly. Voluntary tips are allowed — asking for payment is a demotion-worthy offence.

ℹ️ Extra Info

Always maintain professionalism in ticket channels. For quota questions, check quota-info.
