Privacy Protocol

1. Data Acquisition

We only collect essential Discord identity data (User ID, Username) required to facilitate ticket coordination and anti-fraud monitoring within our carry network.

2. Information Usage

Your data is used solely for identifying you within the ticket system, managing your vouch history, and processing helper applications. We do not sell or share this data with external third parties.

3. Secure Storage

All sensitive operational data is stored securely using industry-standard protocols. Verification data is handled through official Discord OAuth2 systems to ensure maximum security.

4. Data Retention

User metrics and ticket logs are archived for history and accountability purposes. You may request data removal by contacting a network administrator via the Support widget.
